# Experiment-1 Demonstrate the Characteristics of Zener diode.
# Aim:
To plot the V-I characteristics of Zener diode.
# APPARATUS REQUIRED:
![Exp 1 apparatus](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8d7f0fff-649e-4082-b71a-dc02fd6aa82e)

# PROCEDURE:

FORWARD BIAS:

i) The connections are made as per the circuit diagram.

ii) The power supply is switched on.

iii) The forward voltage Vf is increased in small steps and readings are tabulated.

iv) A graph is drawn between Vf and If.

v) The slope of linear portion of graph gives forward resistance and Q point is located.

REVERSE BIAS:

i) The connections are made as per the circuit diagram.

ii) Multimeter current ranges are used to measure the reverse current.

iii) The power supply is switched on and supply voltage is gradually increased.

FORMULA USED:

 Forward Resistance RF = ΔVF / ΔIF (Ω)

Reverse Resistance RR = ΔVR / ΔIR (Ω)
